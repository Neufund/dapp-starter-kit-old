Welcome to Neufund!

We wil help you invest in Neufund.

This should take only a few minutes, and we will walk you trough it.

Step 1: Connect to the Ethereum network

What you do: Wait for the app to connect.

Step 2a: Connect to your hardware wallet

What you need: Either Chrome, Opera or Firefox with plugin destop browser.
What you do:

  • Connect your ledger Nano S
  • Provide the pin (5555 for test nano)
  • Open an ethereum App
Troubleshooting:
  • If u2f version is not showing - make sure you are using Chrome/Opera/Firefox with u2f extension
  • If wallet version is not showing - make sure the ethereum app is opened on your nano
  • Contract function calls should be allowed. If not - change the settings on your nano
  • Wallet app version should be at least 1.0.4

What we do: We poll for the device presence.

Step 2b: Open your hardware wallet

What you need: Either Chrome, Opera or Firefox with plugin destop browser.
What you do: Connect your Ledger Nano S to USB
What we do: keep the balance updated as you faucet and/or invest
